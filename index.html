Application Overview

A centralized platform for booking appointments with private hospitals in Sharjah. Users can:

  • View a list of all available private hospitals.
  • Browse different medical departments and specialties within a selected hospital.
  • View doctor profiles, including their expertise and availability.
  • Select an available time slot for an appointment.
  • Automatically sync their booked appointments with Google Calendar.
  • See nearby hospitals on a map for convenience.
  • Verify insurance coverage and estimated costs before booking.
  • Leave feedback on their booking experience.
